-> How??
    - 2 Important Terms:
        1. Elements (Tag)
            - Representation of a Content
            - 2 Types of Elements
                1. Block
                    <elementName>
                        ......
                        ......
                    </elementName>

                    eg., div, p, h1 to h6, etc., 

                2. Inline 
                    <elementName />

                    eg., <img />, <input /> etc.,

        2. Attributes
            - Represent the Characteristics of an Element
            - 2 Types of Elements
                1. Block
                    <elementName attributeName = "itsValue" attributeName>
                        ......
                        ......
                    </elementName>

                    eg., div, p, h1 to h6, etc., 

                2. Inline 
                    <elementName  attributeName = "itsValue" attributeName />

                    eg., <img />, <input /> etc.,
